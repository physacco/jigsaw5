Jigsaw5

html5 jigsaw tile puzzle game

Introduction

This is a simple html5 jigsaw tile puzzle game.

  1. Click the "Browse" button to choose a picture from your local file system.
  2. The picture will be cut into many small tiles. These tiles are permuted randomly.
  3. Click the bottom-right tile to start moving.
  4. Try your best to restore the original picture :-)

Tested on Firefox 30.
